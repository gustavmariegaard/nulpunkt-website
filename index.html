about
in mathematics, nulpunkt refers to the neutral point where the axes meet — neither positive nor negative. in physics, it refers to absolute zero: the theoretical lowest possible temperature, where thermal motion comes to a minimum.

in collaboration, nulpunkt represents the same idea: a shared starting point. a place without assumptions, agendas, or predefined solutions. where clarity replaces noise, and decisions can be made based on what actually matters.

because without a defined zero point, all variation becomes noise.
